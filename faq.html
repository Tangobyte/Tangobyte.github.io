Frequently asked questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Sign in on print.hz.nl using your school account and follow the instructions on the site.

How can scan a document at HZ and sent it to your laptop?

Sign into on of the printers using your student card and press Scanning-scan. Follow the instructions on the printer.

How can I buy something on the webshop of HZ?

Visit the webshop via the HZ portal and add any item to your shopping cart. To finalize your purchase follow the instruction on the webshop

Where can I find the latest instruction on what to do when you are sick?

answer

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Go to the selfservice portal and make a reservation to book a projectroom

What are the instruction if you want to park your car at HZ?

Free parking is possible on the car park of PZEM at the poelendaelesingel 10 in middelburg. Due to construction work please keep in mind its not possible to reach HZ via the tunnel.
